About Us

Christians on Campus is a medium-sized Christian Organization composed of graduate and undergraduate students.

We are connected with other Christians in Hyde Park and have Friday night fellowship in some of the homes of students and the commnunity believers. We learn about the Lord through directly studying the Bible, and using helpful supplementary ministry reading to better our understanding, including the teachings of two Christian ministers, Watchman Nee and Witness Lee.




Our Community

We also connect with Christian students from other campuses in the Chicago area such as Univeristy of Illinois in Chicago, DePaul, and Northwestern.

Please join us at our Monday and Wednesday Lunch time Bible studies in Bartlett and Hutch commons, or subscribe to our listhost to find out more information about other activities.
